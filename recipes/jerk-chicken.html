Jerk Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 8 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Tb minced fresh ginger
  • 2 Scotch bonnet or habanero chiles, minced
  • 3/8 cup Salsa Lizano (closest substitutes are HP Sauce or A-1 Sauce)
  • 2 Tb olive oil
  • 2 limes, juiced + zest of 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup rum
  • 4 Tb brown sugar
  • 2 tsp ground allspice
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 3 Tb minced fresh thyme
  • Salt to taste (about 2.5 tsp)

Steps

  1. Toss 3 lbs chicken pieces with marinade, and marinate for 2-4 hours, turning once. Grill. Grill some pineapple while you're at it.
  2. Serve with coconut rice (1 14-oz can coconut milk, 1.25 cups water, 1.5 cups rice) and black beans.

Enjoy!
